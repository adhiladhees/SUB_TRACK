About

Our platform is designed to simplify how users manage their digital subscriptions by turning scattered email notifications into clear, actionable insights. By securely analyzing recent subscription-related emails, the system automatically identifies active services, renewal dates, and billing details, and organizes everything into a single, easy-to-use dashboard. This approach helps users stay informed, avoid missed renewals, and reduce unnecessary spending without the need for manual tracking. Built with a strong focus on privacy, transparency, and user control, the platform uses read-only email access and allows manual entry for subscriptions that cannot be detected automatically. A modern, minimal interface with intelligent alerts and spending insights ensures that users not only see their subscriptions but also understand their impact over time.
